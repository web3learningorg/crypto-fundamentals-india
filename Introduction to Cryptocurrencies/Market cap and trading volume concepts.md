# Understanding Market Cap and Trading Volume in Crypto: A Beginner's Guide

Crypto investors must grasp **market cap** and **trading volume** to make smarter decisions. Market cap reflects the total value of a cryptocurrency, indicating stability and adoption, while trading volume measures activity and liquidity, revealing market sentiment and potential price trends.  

This beginner-friendly guide explains how to calculate market cap, the differences between circulating and fully diluted supply, and how volume interacts with market cap to highlight opportunities or risks.
